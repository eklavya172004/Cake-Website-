# Cake Upload Feature - Vendor Quick Start Guide

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Complete Your Onboarding
1. Log in to your vendor dashboard
2. Click on **"Onboarding"** in the sidebar
3. Fill in your business details
4. Submit for review
5. Wait for admin approval (you'll receive an email)

### Step 2: Access the Upload Page
Once approved, you can upload cakes:
1. Click **"Upload Cake"** in the sidebar menu
2. Or click the **"Add New Cake"** button on the Dashboard

### Step 3: Fill in Cake Details

#### Required Information
- **Cake Name** - Name of your cake (e.g., "Chocolate Truffle Cake")
- **Category** - Choose the most relevant category
- **Base Price** - Starting price in rupees
- **Flavors** - Add at least 1 flavor (click "Add" to add multiple)
- **Toppings** - Add at least 1 topping option (click "Add" to add multiple)
- **Images** - Upload 1-4 images (click the upload area to select files)

#### Optional Information
- **Description** - Tell customers about your cake
- **Available Sizes** - Set custom sizes and prices (or use defaults)
- **Customizable** - Check this if customers can customize the cake

### Step 4: Upload Images
1. Click on the upload area
2. Select 1-4 images from your computer
3. Wait for previews to appear
4. Remove any image by clicking the X button
5. Maximum 4 images allowed per cake

### Step 5: Submit
1. Click **"Upload Cake"** button
2. Wait for the upload to complete
3. You'll see a success message
4. Your cake is now live!

## View Your Cakes

### On Dashboard
1. Go to **Dashboard** (/vendor)
2. Scroll down to **"Your Cakes Catalog"** section
3. See all your uploaded cakes with:
   - Cake image
   - Name and category
   - Price
   - Flavors and toppings
   - Number of images

### On Products Page
1. Click **"Manage Cakes"** in the sidebar
2. View all your cakes in a grid layout
3. See cake details at a glance

## Edit Your Cakes

1. Find the cake you want to edit
2. Click the **"Edit"** button (blue button)
3. Update any information you want to change
4. Click **"Update Cake"**

**Note:** Edit functionality will be available soon. For now, you can delete and re-upload.

## Delete Your Cakes

1. Find the cake you want to delete
2. Click the **"Delete"** button (red button)
3. Confirm the deletion when prompted
4. The cake is immediately removed

**Important:** Deletion cannot be undone!

## Troubleshooting

### "Verification Required" Message
**Problem:** You see a yellow box saying your profile needs verification
**Solution:** 
1. Go to "Onboarding" page
2. Complete all required fields
3. Wait for admin approval (24-48 hours usually)

### "Maximum 4 cakes allowed" Error
**Problem:** You see this when trying to upload
**Solution:** 
1. Go to Manage Cakes page
2. Delete a cake you don't need
3. Try uploading again

### "At least 1 image is required" Error
**Problem:** Upload fails without images
**Solution:**
1. Click the upload area again
2. Select at least 1 image
3. Ensure it's a valid image file (PNG, JPG, GIF)

### Image Upload Issues
**Problem:** Images won't upload or take too long
**Solution:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Reduce image file size (compress if large)
3. Try a different image
4. Try again in a few moments

### Form Not Submitting
**Problem:** Upload button doesn't work
**Solution:**
1. Check all required fields are filled
2. Ensure you have images selected
3. Refresh the page
4. Try a different browser

## Best Practices

### Cake Names
- Be descriptive (e.g., "Dark Chocolate Truffle Cake" instead of "Cake")
- Include key ingredients if relevant
- Keep it under 50 characters

### Descriptions
- Tell customers what's special about your cake
- Mention key ingredients (chocolate, vanilla, etc.)
- Highlight dietary options (eggless, sugar-free, etc.)
- Include preparation time if relevant

### Pricing
- Research competitor prices
- Consider ingredient costs
- Price should reflect quality
- You can set different prices for different sizes

### Flavors & Toppings
- List realistic options you can provide
- Be specific (e.g., "Dark Chocolate" not just "Chocolate")
- Only list what you consistently offer
- Examples:
  - Flavors: Chocolate, Vanilla, Strawberry, Eggless Chocolate
  - Toppings: Sprinkles, Almonds, Fresh Berries, Chocolate Chips

### Images
- Use high-quality, well-lit photos
- First image is the main display image
- Show the cake from different angles
- Upload in multiple images to showcase your cake
- Keep images consistent in style
- No watermarks or text overlays
